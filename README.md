# Vigia BR

Vigia BR is a product expiration monitoring system designed to support convenience store operations and reduce financial losses caused by expired products.

## Problem

Convenience stores handle a large volume of perishable items every day.  
Without proper monitoring of expiration dates, many products end up being discarded, generating **financial losses and unnecessary waste**.

Managing this process manually is difficult and often leads to missed opportunities to sell items before they expire.

## Solution

Vigia BR helps store teams track product expiration dates and take action before items expire.

The system monitors expiration dates, identifies items close to their deadline, and suggests actions such as promotional discounts to accelerate sales.

In addition, the system sends **automatic alerts directly to the manager's WhatsApp**, allowing the team to react quickly and prevent losses.

## Features

- Product registration with expiration date
- Dashboard with items close to expiration
- Identification of products at risk
- Promotion suggestions for items near expiration
- Automatic alerts sent via WhatsApp
- Simple web interface for store management
